Star Players: Smart Playlist Management

Transform your playlist experience with Star Players - where your favorite tracks take center stage.

Elevate Your Listening Experience

Star Players introduces a smarter way to manage your playlists. No more creating duplicate playlists just to separate your favorites - now you can mark your top picks with a star and switch between playing all tracks or just your starred selections.

One playlist, two experiences - it's that simple.

Quick Guide

  • Click the star icon on any song to mark it as a favorite
  • Use shuffle to mix up your playlist
  • StarPlay mode plays only your starred songs
