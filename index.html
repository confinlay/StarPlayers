Conor Finlay: Spotify Internship Application

When you curate a playlist, every song has its place, whether it's for background ambiance or your absolute favorites that never fail to get you singing along.

Yet, we've all been there—scrolling through our playlists, seeking out those favorites for a quick mood boost, or when we want to share our top picks with friends.

The other option? You create multiple versions of the same playlist: one for those times when you need an extensive mix, and another pared-down to just the essentials.

What if you didn't have to duplicate playlists anymore?

What if you could have your cake and eat it too—enjoy the full array of your carefully chosen tunes and, with a simple tap, switch to a list of your favorites within that very playlist?

Say goodbye to playlist redundancy and hello to effortless control.

Your favorite tracks are now just a star away from taking center stage, without the clutter of multiple playlists.

With this new feature, it's your music, your rules—enjoy it your way, every time.

Introducing: Star Players

As part of my application for Spotify, I've developed this (very) simple web app to introduce my feature concept: "Star Players". Think of it as a VIP-section for your playlists.

This feature lets you take the reins and choose your personal hits from any playlist you've curated.

In the app demo below, you'll find the usual music player functions: play, pause, skip, and shuffle.

Here's the new headliner: the StarPlay button.

It works alongside the shuffle function, allowing you to toggle between playing all songs or just your 'Star Players'—the songs you've marked as your top picks.

With StarPlay, you're the DJ, curating the beats that deserve the spotlight on your personal stage.

PS: Rest easy, all tunes here are copyright-free and ready for your mixing.

How to Use the App

  • Click the icon on songs to add them to Star Players.
  • Press the StarPlay button to shuffle only your Star Players.
  • Use the regular play, pause, next, and previous controls to navigate your music.
