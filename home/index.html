Project Overview: Star Players

The "Star Players" feature was conceived to address a common issue in digital music platforms—playlist redundancy. I noticed in my own use that maintaining separate playlists, one bloated for variety and another trimmed with only my favorite tracks for specific occasions, was inefficient and cumbersome.

This led to the development of "Star Players", a feature that enhances the music listening experience by allowing users to dynamically manage their favorite tracks within any playlist. This approach eliminates the need to maintain multiple versions of the same playlist, streamlining the user's interaction with their music library.

Within the music player interface, alongside standard controls like play, pause, skip, and shuffle, the "StarPlay" button integrates seamlessly. This button enables users to toggle between playing all songs or just their 'Star Players'—the tracks they have marked as top picks. It offers a personalized and intuitive solution for managing music during different listening scenarios, whether for long background sessions or short, curated experiences with friends.

All features have been implemented using copyright-free materials to ensure that the demo can be freely used and shared without legal constraints.

How to Use the App

  • Click the icon on songs to add them to Star Players.
  • Press the StarPlay button to shuffle only your Star Players.
  • Use the regular play, pause, next, and previous controls to navigate your music.
